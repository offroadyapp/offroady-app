# Offroady Community Constitution

*(Draft v1.2 — Keep it simple, keep it real)*

---

## 🌍 What this community is about

Offroady is built for one simple thing:

> Find a trail → find some good people → go have some fun 🚙

This is not a generic social network.  
This is a **community for off-roaders who actually want to go out and drive**.

---

## 🤝 Core Principles

### 1. Real people, real trips

We’re here to get outside, not just chat.

- Conversations should lead to real trips
- Invite > random messaging
- Respect people’s time and intent

---

### 2. Be respectful

Simple rule:

> Talk to others the way you’d talk at the trailhead.

No harassment, no pressure, no weird behavior.

---

### 3. Consent matters

Just because someone is visible does NOT mean:

- they want to chat with everyone
- they want to join your trip
- they owe you a reply

Respect silence. Move on.

---

### 4. Keep it relevant

This is an off-road community.

Good topics:

- trails
- trips
- vehicles
- gear
- planning

Not for:

- spam
- unrelated promotion
- random cold messaging

---

## 👀 Visibility & Discovery

By default, new users are **visible**.

That means:

- other members can find you in the community
- you may receive invites or messages

You can switch to **hidden anytime**.

When hidden:

- you won’t appear in the community list
- you won’t receive new invites or messages

👉 You are always in control.

**Important:**  
Choosing to hide your profile **does not affect your subscriptions or notifications**.  
You will still receive:

- email updates you subscribed to (such as weekly digest)
- important notifications related to your trips or account

**Also important:**  
Being visible **does not mean you are available or interested in every invitation**.  
Visibility simply allows others to find you — participation is always your choice.

---

## 💬 Messaging Rules (Anti-Spam System)

To keep things clean and respectful:

### Daily limits

- You can contact **up to 5 new people per day**

---

### No message spamming

- If someone hasn’t replied, **do not keep sending messages**
- One message is enough

---

### Invite first, message second

Whenever possible:

> Use **Trip Invite** instead of cold messaging

It gives context and makes things easier for everyone.

---

### Respect boundaries

If someone:

- doesn’t reply
- declines your invite
- blocks you

👉 That’s the end of the conversation.

---

## 🚫 Abuse & Misuse

The following are NOT allowed:

- harassment or aggressive behavior
- repeated unwanted messages
- spam or mass outreach
- misleading profiles
- anything that makes others uncomfortable

---

## 🛑 Block & Report

You are always in control of your experience.

You can:

- **Block** any user (they can no longer contact you)
- **Mute** conversations
- **Report** inappropriate behavior

---

## 🧠 Use Common Sense

If you’re not sure whether something is okay, ask yourself:

> Would this feel normal if we met in person at a trailhead?

If the answer is no — don’t do it.

---

## 🏁 Final Note

Offroady is just a tool.

What really matters is:

- the people you meet
- the trips you go on
- the experiences you share

Keep it simple. Keep it respectful.  
And most importantly—

> Get out there and drive. 🚙
