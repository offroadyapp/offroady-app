<https://offroady-app.vercel.app/weekly-digests/trail-of-the-week-2026-04-20-borden-creek-fsr>

One small tip:
when you sign up, it helps a lot if you add your vehicle after your display name. That makes it easier for other members to know who’s driving what when planning a trip.

For example:

- Mike | Tacoma
- Kevin | Jeep Wrangler
- Lily | 4Runner

And no worries, you can edit it any time later.

If you know someone who’d enjoy it, feel free to share a trail or trip and get a run going 🙂

See you out there,
